Checkbox focus


Checkbox States
Click on Focus button and use spacebar to toggle checkbox.
If focus isn't on checkbox, spacebar has no effect.

<alloy-checkbox #focusCheckBox [label]="placeholder"
    [disabled]="disabled" [checked]="checked">
    [readonly]="readonlyFocus" [errors]="errorsFocus"
    [errorMessage]="errorMessage"
</alloy-checkbox>

@ViewChild('focusCheckBox') alloyCheckBox: AlloyCheckBox;

or

@ViewChild('focusCheckBox') alloyCheckBox: FocusableOption;

alloyCheckBox.focus();
